# 🎵 Music Player

> A modern **Music Player** built with **JavaScript** and **CSS**.  
> Enjoy adding your favorite tracks to playlists, drag & drop to reorder, and view detailed information for each song.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- ➕ **Add Music to Playlist** – Organize your favorite tracks easily  
- ⏩⏪ **Skip Forward & Backward** – Navigate through songs smoothly  
- 🖱 **Drag & Drop** – Rearrange playlist order with simple drag & drop  
- 🔍 **View Song Details** – Check track info like artist, album, and duration  
- 🎨 **Responsive Design** – Works well on both desktop and mobile devices
